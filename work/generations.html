Gerações em Movimento - Logo

Client:

Gerações em Movimento project

The "Gerações em Movimento" project (which can be loosely translated to “Moving Generations”) is a social, territorial, generational and cultural intervention and promotion project promoted by various social institutions.

Brief:

They needed a logo that represented the spririt of the project and that could be used accross a variety of contexts.

Result:

The identity uses the element of a tree whose leaves change colour to convey the metaphor of generational complementarity.

The logo can be used in three different formats, maintaining some freshness and grace and allowing the logo to work in different contexts.

Starting from the idea of the continuity of generations and movement, the main colours are blue and orange. These colours are complementary to each other, a bit like the different generations that follow, guaranteeing the ideal contrast and compatibility.
