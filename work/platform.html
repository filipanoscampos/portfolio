Central Alentejo Cultural and Creative Platform Redesign

Client:

CIMAC

CIMAC, the intermunicipal community of central Alentejo, had built an online platform to showcase cultural and creative projects of the region.

Brief:

Unhappy with how their old websites looked, they launched a public tender for a complete redesign of the platform's appearence.
Mine was the chosen proposal.

Result:

I optimized the website by working from the existing structure.

I redesigned the whole introductory section.

Without removing textual information, I made the sections for each type of information clearer and more appealing.

The users are welcomed in a friendly manner with the confirmation that they are in the right place.

The ecossystem map is more appealing and easy to use and similar elements accross the website behave similarly.

This website also has a management tool for cultural organizations where they can add events and places to the ecossystem.

The users were very happy with the changes and found the website a lot more appealing and easy to use. After the redesign the number of registered users and events grew considerably.
