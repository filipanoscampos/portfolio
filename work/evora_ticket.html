Évora Ticket Interaction Design

Client:

Decsis - Information Systems

Decsis contacted me to design the structure and interaction of what was going to become the Évora Ticket website. A website where the user can purchase a ticket that gives access to many touristic attractions of the city of Évora.

Brief:

A public tender had been launched to select a communication agency to design the identity for the new ticket and all related materials.

Decsis wanted to make sure the interaction and information structure was defined in order to guarantee that the website would be user friendly and the selected team coud use my mockups to apply graphic styles.

Result:

A user friendly website that works accross all platforms and screen sizes.

From the backoffice structure, defined user roles and use cases, I designed the website mockup and defined optimal behaviours for elements. The grapgic design team could then use my documents to atribute visual styles which were then brought back to me to be refined and given the necessary CSS.

The wonderful três agás team eventually won the public tender for the graphic image.

From the website the user can purchase a ticket to many of the city's attractions and also found information about the city and the region.
