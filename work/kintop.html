Kintop Website

Client:

Kintop

A film production company, mostly dedicated to documentary films. They pride themselves for the care they bring to each project.

Brief:

Kintop wanted a new website where they could show all relevant information on completed and ongoing projects.

Result:

A clean website that revolves around large image sliders. .

Each film has its own page with detailed information organized through tabs (desktop version) or accordions (mobile version).
